Getting Started

1. How do I begin to use schooltek?

After contacting and recivig an email with your ID, password and Login, you can upload your school information within schooltek.

2. How do I connect with parents and students?

Please upload your school information including the list of e-mails from your professors, parents and students. Then they will receive ande mail with their ID, password and login to use schooltek. The will also recive a link to download the app from the Apple App store or from Google Play. After installing the app, they can begin to use schooltek in their Smartphone. They acan also Access to schooltek by the web.

Security

1. How is the schooltek app secure?

The Schooltek app uses encryption and secure connections to keep your data secure. And all your comunity will use a unique ID from your school. Any other people from other school can connect to your school information.

Communicating

1. Schooltek apps can send and receive messages to/from the parents and students?

Only the schooltek app of parents and students can be used to receive messages from the school. They can only see notifications and confirm to schools of been received messages or been informed ot one event in their calendar.

2. Which network does the schooltek use?

The schooltek can be used by wifi or data plan depending of your internet provider.

3. Does my phone need to be on a specific network?

No. Your phone can be on any network.

4. Can I make voice calls to/from the schooltek?

It is not possible to make voice calls from/to the schooltek.

5. To whom can parentes and students send confirmation messages from the schooltek?

Parents can see the information related to their children (more than one) in one particular school and they can send messages abour confirmation the information they received to the school.

6. From whom can my parents and studentes receive messages on the schooltek?

Parents and student will receive messages from teachers or the people resonsible for schooltek in your school.

Apps and use

1. How often do I need to dowload schooltek?

You can download any time the app and the upgrades will be free on charge.

2. How do kids use schooltek?

Kids can use the schooltek app to connect with school, to track their academic information.

3. Who is schooltek designed for?

Schooltek is designed for schools from elementary to miclle school.

4. Which smartphones are supported?

Schooltek supports iOS (iPhone 5, 5S, 6, 6+, 6S and 6S+) and Android phones starting with version 4.1 (Jelly Beam) which includes Samsung, LG, Motorola, HTC and others.

5. What happens if the schooltek app is removed?

You can download the app any time
